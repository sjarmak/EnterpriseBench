# Idea 6: Onboarding Knowledge Transfer Simulation

Give the agent a large OSS codebase (Kubernetes, React, PostgreSQL) and simulate a new-hire onboarding scenario: 'You're joining the networking team, you need to understand how pod-to-pod traffic works.' The agent produces a structured knowledge document. Scoring: a panel of ACTUAL maintainers of that project rates the document's accuracy and completeness via pre-recorded rubrics (extracted from real onboarding docs, ADRs, and design docs). This measures holistic codebase comprehension — not finding a specific file, but building a correct mental model. Enterprise workflow: every new hire does this.
