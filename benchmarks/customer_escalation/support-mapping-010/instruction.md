# Support Ticket: Chat rooms always show as unread even after reading everything

**Priority:** Medium
**Submitted by:** Office Manager
**Product:** Element Web (self-hosted)

---

Hi there,

We have an annoying problem with our Element chat. Several rooms always show a bold unread indicator (the dot or number badge) even after we've read every single message in the room. We scroll all the way to the bottom, read everything, but the room still shows as unread in the sidebar.

It seems to happen more in rooms where people use threads a lot. If I open a room that only has regular messages, the unread indicator clears properly. But rooms with active threads seem to get "stuck" as unread no matter what we do.

We've tried marking rooms as read manually, refreshing the page, even logging out and back in. Sometimes it clears temporarily but comes back within minutes.

This is really frustrating because we can't tell at a glance which rooms actually have new messages. Everyone on the team is complaining about it. We end up having to click into every room to check, which defeats the purpose of the unread indicators.

Can you help us understand what controls the unread state for rooms, especially when threads are involved?

Thanks,
